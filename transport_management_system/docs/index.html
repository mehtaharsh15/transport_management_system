Provide solution for daily transportation activity

Track daily Transport activity, record data of customer, location, Charges of delivery etc

Generate Report and send it to Company for billing purpose

Install

From your site

To install this app, login to your site and click on "Installer". Search for Transport Management System and click on "Install"

Using Bench

Go to your bench folder and setup the new app

$ bench get-app transport_management_system https://github.com/vishdha/transport_management_system
$ bench new-site testsite
$ bench --site testsite install-app transport_management_system

Login to your site to configure the app.
